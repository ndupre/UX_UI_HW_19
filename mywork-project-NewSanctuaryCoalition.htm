NOP Website Redesign

The New Sanctuary Coalition

Redesign of the website to increase public trust and engagement trough volunteer oportunities and donation transparency.

Redesign of the New Sanctuary Coalition website, non profit fighting against deportations and advocationg for immigrats rights in NYC. The goal was to highlight their work and make it easier for people to get involved in their cause.

Role & Responsbilities

This project involved 2 UX designers, we followed the Design thinking methodology.

Deliverables

User Interviews, Protopersona, Survey, Affinity Diagram, Heuristic Analysis, Prioritization Matrix, User Persona, User Flows, Low fidelity prototype, User testing, UI style Guide, Hi-Fidelity Prototype, Google Slides

Tools

Miro, Figma, Adobe XD, Otter, Excel, Photoshop, Google Slides, Pen and paper.

Timeline & Scope

3 weeks to design 2 flows: Volunteer Sign Up and Donations

  • Research
  • Ideation
  • Definition
  • Prototyping
  • Testing

    • Research

    To start we designed a research plan and interviewed 6 user with the goal to identify how people get involved and in what capacity, what are their motivations, needs and painpoint.Discovering that users need to feel the organization is trustworthy indicating transparency of fundings, donnors and their work structure as main factors to decide to invest time or money in the cause.
