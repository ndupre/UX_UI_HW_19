Hi, I'm Nicole Dupre

Chilean-born UX Designer living in Brooklyn, NY since 2012. My work focus on placing users at the center, by taking a data-driven approach to create seamless and meaningful interactions.

My Skills

Empathy & Awareness

Gain understanding of the users needs and painpoints is a key part my process, having bast experience designing User Research and User Testing. Implementing Surveys, conducting Benchmarks and Competitive Analysis, to design experiences based on quantitative and qualitative information.

Ideation

Having my user persona on hand and the problem identified, I generate possible solutions and prioritize them depending on their impact and faceability. With this I can estate the user journey using different methods as storyboards, empathy maps or user journey maps.

Prototyping

I’m skilled defining user flows, often taking them to quick paper prototypes and Lo-fidelity wireframes to test and iterate till defining Hi-fidelity mocks.

My experience

I graduated from the Design School of Universidad Católica of Valparaíso in 2008, being my main focus of study Interaction Design, Information Architecture and Usability Studies. As a continuation of my thesis work, I co-founded Humming, partnering with a software company, working on CMS, websites and apps for retail and hospitals. After realizing that I was craving for mentorship, I decided to depart the venture and started working as a junior UI Designer on a digital content network. From then, I explore different areas of User Experience Design. This led me to work as a UX Consultant for banks, retail, digital campaigns, and government institutions among others.

My Interests

Other than creating services that make peoples lives easier, I’m deeply passionate about creating systemic change. The flexibility of my consultant work has given me the freedom to study food systems, do organizing work at my local community garden @GreeneAcres. And for the past two growing seasons to farm at the Brooklyn Grange, the biggest soil-based rooftop farm in the world. This year I grew literally thousands of plants that went to community gardens all around NYC.
I’m also the proud mama of a 11 years old that is the world’s most kind and creative person, I swear I’m not bias! They make me think a lot on how to make the world less oppressive for children, so they can be their full selves. So I’m part of The Brooklyn Freeschool community and a board member.
