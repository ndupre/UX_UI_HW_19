Hi, I'm Nicole Dupre

Ux Designer specialized in Interaction Design.

I’m Nicole Dupre, I’m a Chilean-born UX Designer living in Brooklyn, NY science 2012. I studied Graphic Design at the School of Design of Universidad Catolica de Valparaíso, Chile. I focus on a data-driven approach through research to design seamless and meaningful interactions.
