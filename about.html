Hi, I'm Nicole Dupre

I’m a Chilean-born UX Designer living in Brooklyn, NY since 2012. I studied Graphic Design at the School of Design of Universidad Catolica de Valparaíso, Chile. My work focus on placing users at the center, by taking a data-driven approach to create seamless and meaningful interactions.

My experience

I graduated in 2008, being my main focus on Information Architecture and Usability Studies. As a contnuation of my thesis work, I co-founded Humming, partnering with a software company, working on CMS, websites and apps for retail and hospitals. After realizing that I was craving for mentorship, I decided to depart the venture working as a junior UI Designer and from then explore different areas of User Experience Design. This led me to work as a UX Consultant for banks, retail, Digital Campaigns, and Government institutions among others.

My Interests

Other than creating services that make peoples lives easier, I’m deeply passionate about creating systemic change. The flexibility of my consultant work has given me the freedom to study food systems, do organizing work at my local community garden @GreeneAcres. And for the past two growing seasons to farm at the Brooklyn Grange, the biggest soil-based rooftop farm in the world.
